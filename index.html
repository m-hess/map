Explore my map of fake data!
The map is generated using Mapbox GL, and the data was generated randomly through json-generator.com/.
Here are some built-in tools to check out and how to use them:

  • Scrolling:
    Use your trackpad or mouse to change the zoom level of the map by scrolling
  • Clustering:
    Try changing the zoom level to see how points automatically cluster to simplify the view.
  • Fly-To Zoom:
    Try clicking on one of the clusters to zoom in on a point.
  • Search Bar:
    Try plugging an address into the search bar on the top right. The map will change the view and zoom in on the address.
  • Popups:
    Try zooming in on a point until it becomes a small blue dot. When it does, click on it! You will see a random address displayed in a popup window.
  • Polygonal Selection Tool:
    Use the button in the top right corner of the screen to get the area of a selected region.
